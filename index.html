About the company

The Code Company is a globally minded, Sofia based operation. Our main job is the development of software products. We specialize in ERP and CRM systems, custom IT solutions tailor-made for the client’s needs, apps, online-shops and websites. We strive to exceed our clients’ expectations by providing elegant and efficient end-results, seamlessly integrating design and functionality. Our work is mostly in the Health Care & Hospital/Clinic, Automotive, Non-Profit, Architecture, Charity, Consumer goods and Cosmetics sectors but we are eager to expand. We also provide in-house Marketing services ranging from online advertising to creation of logos, custom designs and photo shoots.
